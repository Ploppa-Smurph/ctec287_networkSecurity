02) A project manager has designed a new secure data center and has decided to use multifactor locks on each door to prevent unauthorized access. Compare the
       following types of locks that the project manager may use to determine which example the facility is utilizing.
    A lock that requires an employee to use a smart card and pin to enter.

        - A multifactor lock is one that may combine different methods to enter, such as a smart card with a PIN.
    
03) An organization plans the destruction of old HDDs. In an effort to save money, the organization decides that the technicians will hit the drives with hammers. 
       Which method is being used?
    Pulverizing - Pulverizing involves destroying media by impact. It is important to note that hitting a hard drive with a hammer can actually leave a surprising
         amount of recoverable data. Industrial machinery should perform this type of destruction.
        
04) The security team at an organization looks to protect highly confidential servers. Which method does the team propose when protecting the servers against explosives?
    vault - A vault is a room that is hardened against unauthorized entry by physical means, such as drilling or explosives.
